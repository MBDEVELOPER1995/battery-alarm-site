Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Battery Alarm ("the app") handles information and explains the purpose of the permissions it requires. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to transparency about our practices.

This app is designed to function without collecting or transmitting any of your personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Battery Alarm does not collect, store, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Location data
  • Contacts

All app settings and user preferences are stored exclusively on your device. The app processes non-personal technical data such as battery level, charging status, and temperature strictly to provide its core functionality. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

Battery Alarm uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • IP address (may be used for approximate location)
  • Device information such as model and operating system
  • Ad interaction and performance data

We do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob. For more information, please review Google’s policies:

Permissions Required and Their Purpose

Battery Alarm requests the following permissions strictly to enable its core features:

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE
    Allows continuous battery monitoring in the background so the alarm can trigger at the exact user-defined charge level.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS
    Required to show persistent monitoring notifications and critical alarm alerts.
  • VIBRATE
    Used to provide vibration alerts when the alarm is triggered.
  • CAMERA & FLASHLIGHT
    Used exclusively for the optional Flashlight Alert feature, which blinks the device flashlight as a visual alarm.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
    Allows the app to restart battery monitoring automatically after device reboot.
  • WAKE_LOCK & USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT
    Enables the app to wake the device and display the alarm over the lock screen to ensure the user is alerted.
  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
    Required solely for Google AdMob to load and display advertisements. The core alarm functionality works offline.

Data Security

All app-related data, including alarm settings, is stored locally on your device and protected by the standard security mechanisms of the Android operating system.

Children’s Privacy

Battery Alarm is not intended for use by children under the age of 9. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
